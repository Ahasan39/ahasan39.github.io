## üé® Visual Style Guide

### Color Scheme
- **Background**: Dark theme `hsl(222 47% 6%)` - Professional dark blue
- **Primary Accent**: Teal/Cyan `hsl(172 66% 50%)` - Modern, tech-forward
- **Text**: Light foreground `hsl(210 40% 98%)` - High contrast
- **Gradients**: Teal to cyan gradients for highlights
- **Glow Effects**: Subtle teal glow on interactive elements

### Typography
- **Headings**: Sora font (Bold, 600-800 weight)
- **Body**: Inter font (Regular, 400-500 weight)
- **Sizes**: Large, readable text for video format

### Animation Principles
- **Smooth**: All transitions use ease-in-out curves
- **Professional**: No jarring or excessive animations
- **Purposeful**: Every animation serves to guide viewer attention
- **Consistent**: 0.3s - 0.5s duration for most transitions
